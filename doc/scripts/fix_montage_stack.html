Script fix_montage_stack

Removes SerialEM Montage piece coordinates from image extended header.

This is a program to erase the montage piece coordinates from the extended header of a tilt-series, which somehow makes its way into random tilt-series collected by SerialEM.

fix_montage_stack (input_filename, output_filename)
Removes montage piece coordinates from MRC extended header. Removes the flags for montage piece coordinates from the header and then erases the piece coordinates stored in the extended header and copies this new header to the tilt-series.

Parameters:

  • input_filename initial tilt-series e.g. 'image.st'
  • output_filename corrected tilt-series e.g. 'image_fixed.st'
